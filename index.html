Qubo

Cronyx's newest game has been in development since May of 2018. It is a pseudo-sokoban style puzzle game taking inspiration from Fez and Bloxorz. In our game, you will attempt to rotate various 3-dimensional blocks into a particular orientation by manipulating the game's world in two dimensions. The game's 3-dimensional geometry is flattened into 2-dimensions, allowing movement that seems to defy reason. This mechanic will force you to reimagine the way you move and explore the space around you.

With 14 worlds and 72 levels to explore, the game is not for the faint of heart. Are you up to the challenge?

Find more information about the game, including links to Steam, Google Play, and the App Store, at qubo's website. For any additional information, email us at info@cronyxgames.com. See you on the flip side!
